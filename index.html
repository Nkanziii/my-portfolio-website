About
Me

I'm Niki Khanezai — a creative coder and front-end developer who builds expressive, interactive experiences on the web. I'm drawn to the intersection of art and code — it's where I find the most innovative ideas are born. That curiosity drives everything I make, whether it's generative art that responds to real-world data, 3D web experiences that pull you in, or physical computing that bridges the digital and the tangible. I work primarily in JavaScript and p5.js, with experience across Three.js, Arduino & C++, Unity, TouchDesigner, and projection mapping.

Particle Weather

A live weather visualisation built with vanilla JavaScript and p5.js. I fetched real-time atmospheric data from the Open-Meteo API and mapped it to a generative particle system — 800 particles orbit the canvas, with their speed set by wind speed and their colour palette switching across six weather states depending on temperature and conditions.
